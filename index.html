Introduction

I am an avid web designer / developer focused on crafting great web experiences. It was a few years ago – four years precisely, when my passion for web design / development aroused.

One fateful day, I scrolled through my phone and found a tutorial book on XHTML 1.0 and I opened it and casually read it. I did not really understand much that day, but my interest sparked.

Ever since, needless to say, I have taken the full leap into this profession: enjoying the art of creating beautifully designed, intuitive and functional websites, with an eye on accessibility.

Where I'm From

I'm originally from Nigeria, but recently immigrated to Toronto, Ontario to pursue a career in web development.
